# Important Info

While the exclusion system is strong, there are some thing that currently cannot be done:

1. You cannot remove the .git exclusion. This is because (and from my personal experience testing this) git's binary nature essentially pseudorandomizes its file and generates a perfect diffmarker that will mess with the engine. I have honestly zero clue on how to fix this.
2. You are unfortunately not able to directly build and modify the three starter exclusions. You can move things out of their folders and such to get around this

That being said: I do have this roadmappped! These issues will be getting fixed.
